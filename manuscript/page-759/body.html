”  The captain persuaded me to accept a suit of clothes newly made; but I would not suffer the tailor to take my measure; however, Don Pedro being almost of my size, they fitted me well enough.  He accoutred me with other necessaries, all new, which I aired for twenty-four hours before I would use them.

The captain had no wife, nor above three servants, none of which were suffered to attend at meals; and his whole deportment was so obliging, added to very good human understanding, that I really began to tolerate his company.  He gained so far upon me, that I ventured to look out of the back window.  By degrees I was brought into another room, whence I peeped into the street, but drew my head back in a fright.  In a week’s time he seduced me down to the door.
