I only desired he would lend me two clean shirts, which, having been washed since he wore them, I believed would not so much defile me.  These I changed every second day, and washed them myself.

We arrived at Lisbon, Nov. 5, 1715.  At our landing, the captain forced me to cover myself with his cloak, to prevent the rabble from crowding about me.  I was conveyed to his own house; and at my earnest request he led me up to the highest room backwards.  I conjured him “to conceal from all persons what I had told him of the Houyhnhnms; because the least hint of such a story would not only draw numbers of people to see me, but probably put me in danger of being imprisoned, or burnt by the Inquisition.
