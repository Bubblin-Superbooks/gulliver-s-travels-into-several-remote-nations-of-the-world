Let me deal so candidly with the reader as to confess that there was yet a much stronger motive for the freedom I took in my representation of things.  I had not yet been a year in this country before I contracted such a love and veneration for the inhabitants, that I entered on a firm resolution never to return to humankind, but to pass the rest of my life among these
